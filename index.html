Logics & Network Institute of Technology (Training & Educational Group)

Logics and Network institute of Technology -Training & Educational Group was established in the year 2015. With the aim to impart the technical and professional education to the society. With mantra "We don't teach we train" (हम पढ़ाते नहीं सिखाते हैं।) Initial aim of our group is to impart the education of skill which leads the student and helps him/her to work in the era of digital age as a professional . We guide our students to begin the career not only as an employee but also as an employer. Initially we started to impart knowledge in the field of Information Technology and Electronics to generate a benchmark in the society of continuosly delivering technocrats in the field of software development, web development , Hardware Networking, Digital Marketing & Cyber security. Later we opted to enhance our social role to work in the field of skill and education in various other sectors recently joining hands with MSME and providing training on ESDP programs and already working as Facilitation centre of NIELIT along with it providing vocational training courses and nurturing other sectors skill programming and training related to various other courses .
